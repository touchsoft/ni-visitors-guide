Things to do in Northern Ireland

Giant's Causeway

Marvel at 40,000 interlocking basalt columns formed by ancient volcanic activity. This UNESCO World Heritage Site combines natural wonder with legendary tales.
Titanic Belfast

Experience the world's largest Titanic exhibition where the legendary ship was built. Interactive galleries bring the story to life.
Carrick-a-Rede Bridge

Cross the famous rope bridge suspended 30 meters above the Atlantic Ocean. Not for the faint of heart, but the views are spectacular.
Dunluce Castle

Explore the dramatic ruins of this medieval castle perched on clifftops, offering spectacular views of the Antrim coastline and the Atlantic Ocean.
Mourne Mountains

Conquer Northern Ireland's highest peaks and explore the stunning granite mountains that inspired C.S. Lewis's Chronicles of Narnia.
Belfast Murals

Discover the world's most famous political street art that tells the story of Belfast's complex history and journey to peace.
Old Bushmills Distillery

Visit the world's oldest licensed whiskey distillery and sample premium Irish whiskey made with traditional methods since 1608.
Glens of Antrim

Explore nine scenic valleys running from the Antrim Mountains to the sea, steeped in Celtic history and natural beauty.
Dark Hedges

Walk through the famous tree tunnel that served as the Kingsroad in Game of Thrones and discover the legend of the Grey Lady.

Come and Dream in the Land of Legends

Northern Ireland offers unforgettable experiences around every corner. From the dramatic coastlines of the Causeway Coast to the vibrant streets of Belfast, discover ancient castles, world-class museums, stunning natural landscapes, and warm hospitality that will make your visit truly memorable.

With its rich history, breathtaking scenery, and welcoming people, Northern Ireland is the perfect destination for those seeking adventure, culture, and natural beauty all in one place.
