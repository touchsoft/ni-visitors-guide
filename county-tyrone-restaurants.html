Where to Eat in County Tyrone

County Tyrone offers exceptional dining from award-winning gastropubs to traditional pubs, perfectly positioned for exploring rural landscapes and experiencing authentic Ulster hospitality.

The Brewer's House

Award-winning gastropub housed in an 18th century building. Winner of Best Gastro Pub and Best Wine Experience at the Irish Restaurant Awards 2018. Focuses on quality ingredients sourced from local suppliers in a historic setting.

O'Brien's Restaurant at Mellon Country Inn

Family-run establishment with breathtaking panoramic countryside views from every seat. Serves food all day from 7am-9pm and is the closest restaurant to Ulster American Folk Park, making it perfect for tourists exploring the area.

Tomneys Bar

At least 300 years old, one of the oldest and most traditional bars in the area. Features original Aga and hosts live traditional music. On the famous Irish Pub trail, described as "like walking into someone's house" for its authentic atmosphere.

Location:

10 The Square, Moy

Type:

Traditional Irish Pub
