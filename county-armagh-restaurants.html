Where to Eat in County Armagh

County Armagh offers delightful dining from award-winning bistros to traditional pubs, perfectly positioned for exploring the spiritual heart of Ireland and the beautiful Orchard County.

Digby's Bar & Restaurant

Award-winning gastropub located on the tree-lined main street of Killylea, just six miles west of Armagh city. This traditional family-run establishment has been operating since 1910, offering excellent cuisine, fine wines, and a relaxing, cozy atmosphere.

Location:

53 Main Street, Killylea

The Hole in the Wall Bar

Historic pub housed in a former jail building, creating a surprisingly cozy atmosphere despite its past. Popular with locals and known for its character-filled environment, live music, and traditional pub atmosphere. Features a beer garden.
