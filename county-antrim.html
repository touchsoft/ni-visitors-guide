Where to Stay in County Antrim

County Antrim offers accommodation options from luxury castle hotels to cozy B&Bs, all perfectly positioned for exploring the Giant's Causeway, Belfast, and the stunning Causeway Coast.

The Causeway Hotel

The closest accommodation to the Giant's Causeway. This Grade II listed hotel offers 28 en-suite rooms, all with views of the Atlantic Ocean or private terraces. Perfect for exploring this UNESCO World Heritage site with free access for guests.

Northern Ireland's only 17th-century castle still in use as a residence, built in 1625. This charming 4-star hotel offers a unique historical experience with easy access to Giant's Causeway, Carrick-a-Rede Bridge, and Game of Thrones filming locations.
