Blakes of the Hollow

Established in 1887, this famous Victorian pub is considered "one of the happiest and most beautiful bars in the whole of Ireland" by novelist John McGahern. Features traditional Irish heritage, Victorian layout, snugs, and live traditional music every Friday.

Location:

6 Church Street, Enniskillen

Type:

Traditional Irish Pub

Phone:

028 6632 2143

Visit Website

Tully Mill Restaurant

A hidden gem nestled in the foothills of Benaughlin and Cuilcagh mountains on the edge of Florence Court Estate. Run by local chef John Roche, offering traditional menu with modern twists using local and seasonal produce.

Location:

Mill Road, Florence Court
